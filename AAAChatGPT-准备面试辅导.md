Wolfsberg Group 支持金融机构使用 AI/ML 来检测、调查和管理金融犯罪风险，但强调要有适当的数据伦理原则，确保结果公平、有效、可解释；其 AI/ML principles 包括 legitimate purpose、proportionate use、design and technical expertise、accountability and oversight、openness、transparency。([Wolfsberg Group](https://wolfsberg-group.org/news/publication-of-the-wolfsberg-principles-for-using-artificial-intelligence-and-machine-learning-in-financial-crime-compliance?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

# 4. LLM vs Machine Learning: how to position them

## English

**Traditional ML** is better for structured prediction problems: risk scoring, anomaly detection, transaction monitoring, classification, prioritization and pattern recognition.

**LLMs** are better for language-heavy and document-heavy problems: summarization, extraction, explanation, policy Q&A, case narrative generation and analyst assistance.

The best solution is often a combination: ML provides scoring and detection, while LLMs help explain, summarize and guide workflow actions.

## 中文

**传统 ML** 更适合结构化预测问题，例如风险评分、异常检测、交易监控、分类、优先级排序和模式识别。

**LLM** 更适合语言和文档密集型问题，例如总结、抽取、解释、政策问答、case narrative 生成和分析师辅助。

最好的方案往往是组合使用：  
**ML 负责 scoring / detection，LLM 负责 summarization / explanation / workflow assistance。**

# 5. Open Banking angle

如果面试官说 OB 是 **Open Banking**，可以这样补充：

Open Banking data can enrich KYC if it is collected with proper consent and used under approved controls. # 6. Governance and control points

AI in KYC must be controlled because it touches compliance, client data, financial crime risk and customer access decisions.

Key controls:

- approved data usage
- data quality and lineage
- model validation
- explainability for high-risk decisions
- human-in-the-loop review
- audit trail
- access control
- prompt and output logging for LLM
- model performance monitoring
- bias / fairness review
- fallback process
- periodic model review

NIST’s AI Risk Management Framework organizes AI risk management around **Govern, Map, Measure and Manage**, while MAS’s FEAT principles emphasize fairness, ethics, accountability and transparency for AI/data analytics in financial services. These are useful governance references when discussing AI in banking. ([NIST AI Resource Center](https://airc.nist.gov/airmf-resources/airmf/5-sec-core/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
